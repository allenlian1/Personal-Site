Pancake Rotation

I engineered an experimental setup to study the pancake rotation phenomena, designing and fabricating an orbital shaker capable of achieving precise oscillatory frequencies up to 20 rad/s. Using a GoPro feed and OpenCV, we developed a motion-tracking algorithm to independently track hundreds of spheres in real time. To better understand collision dynamics, we modeled ball-to-ball Hertzian contact mechanics, simulating interactions at 10kHz. The collected tracking data was processed and visualized using Matplotlib, Excel, and Pandas, enabling detailed analysis of orbital translation patterns.
